# History & Heritage

Our home and neighborhood tell a story of growth, preservation, and community—a testament to the enduring spirit of Texas living.

## The Early Years

Built in the early 20th century, this home represents the architectural style typical of Texas homes from that era, featuring design elements perfectly suited to our climate.

## Architectural Legacy

High ceilings for air circulation, large windows for natural light, and a welcoming front porch—each element reflects the practical wisdom of early Texas builders.

## Modern Stewardship

Today, we continue this legacy through thoughtful updates that honor the past while embracing sustainable modern living.
