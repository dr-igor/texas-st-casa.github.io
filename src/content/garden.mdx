# Our Texas Garden

Discover the beautiful plants that thrive in our Texas garden. Each plant has been carefully selected to create a sustainable, water-wise landscape that celebrates Texas's natural beauty.

## Garden Philosophy

We believe in working with nature, not against it. Our garden features drought-tolerant, native and adapted plants that provide year-round interest while requiring minimal maintenance.

## Seasonal Beauty

From the silvery foliage of Texas Sage to the vibrant blooms of Pride of Barbados, our garden offers a constantly changing display of colors and textures throughout the seasons.
